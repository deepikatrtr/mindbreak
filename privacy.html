Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Mindbreak ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Mindbreak is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe your break habits are strictly your business. Therefore:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any inherently personally identifiable information.
  • No Analytics Tracking: We do not monitor your behavior within the App, tracking your breaks or schedule.
  • No Account Required: You can use all features of the App without creating an account.

2. Locally Stored Data

The App requires certain data to function (such as your configured break intervals and notification preferences). This data is saved locally on your device using underlying platform features (like SharedPreferences on Android). We do not transmit or store this data on external servers.

3. Permissions Required

The Mindbreak app may request the following device permissions to function correctly:

  • Notifications: We need to send local notifications to remind you to take your breaks. These notifications are scheduled locally on the device.

4. Third-Party Services

Our App is an offline-first utility. It does not utilize third-party SDKs that collect data on our behalf for advertising or behavioral tracking. However, your device operating system (e.g., Google Play Services) may collect crash reports and diagnostics depending on your device's global analytics settings.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at info@teczosoftwares.com.
